Access Control Principles

●Business Users can access application layers only.
●No direct access to Database from Business Users.
●IT Operations access is via Bastion/PAM with MFA, Approval and Session Logging.
●Least Privilege, Need-to-Know Access.

Important

⊘No direct access to DB from Office Network.
⊘No public access to internal subnets.
⊘All admin access is restricted and auditable.
